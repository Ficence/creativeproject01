Commissions!

Hi my name is Hyrum and I do take art commissions for dragons! I will draw a cartoony dragon to fit your uses to be either your profile picture or to be your desktop background.

I've been traditionally drawing since I was young, and have been a digital artist since 2014. My experience can insure that your requested art will be a unique piece that represents you.

Styles

I have two main styles I can provide:

  1. Digital Sketch, or beginner concept art. I can provide a sketch of your dragon when you provide a description.
  2. Colored. I will do the line art and color your dragon in addition. Shading and lighting will be an additional fee.
